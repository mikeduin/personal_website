What's the Deal? NFL Battle the Bookies is an ultimate test of NFL handicapping ability ... and is not for the weak of heart. In this game, each entrant is required to make a fake $100 bet on each game in the NFL season by betting on one of the four side outcomes (either team's moneyline or point spread) or one of the two total outcomes (over or under the amount of total points scored by both teams). Whoever has accumulated the most fake dollars at the end of the NFL season wins the pool. For a more detailed view of how the game works, please take a look at last year's scorecard (and note the different tabs for each week's selections).
