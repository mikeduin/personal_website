What's the Deal? The NCAA March Madness Pool is A Level Above's most popular game and hosts more than 400 entrants annually. Scoring is traditional, with each correct pick in the first round earning an entrant one (1) point and correct picks doubling in value in each successive round until a correct Championship pick is worth 32 points.
