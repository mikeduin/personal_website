Universal Career Data

24 years. 270+ competitions. 1,600+ entrants. $540,000+ in prizes.

All-time entrant data encompassing every game and competition in A Level Above's twenty-year history can be found here. For each of the ~1,600 entrants that have played in an ALA game, career data includes Total Entries, Championships, Top-3 Finishes, Buy-ins, Prizes, and Net Profits, along with more detailed rankings for all fifteen individual ALA games. Entrants are ranked by total prizes won in their ALA careers, and data will be updated upon the completion of every ALA game.
