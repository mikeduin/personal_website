Buy-ins and Prizes: Explained

Many of ALA's fantasy games and pools utilize a dynamic "choose your buy-in" system to accommodate both the casual and serious gamer (...or gambler). Entrants are eligible to win only a percentage of the prize pool that is proportionate to their buy-in amount.
