What's the Deal? NCAA Bowl Pick 'em carries the format of the NCAA Pick'em game into the Bowl Season. Each entrant makes a pick against the spread for all but the most meaningless of bowl games, and selects five games of their choosing for which to assign double weight (so a correct pick is worth two points instead of one). Correct picks for the two national semifinals and the national championship are worth three points. The game is run manually through a Google Docs workbook that shows entrants' picks as well as the percentage of the pool that is backing each side in each game.
