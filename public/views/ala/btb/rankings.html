Below you will find the current All-Time Rankings for NFL Battle the Bookies. Due to the constraints of embedded Google Sheets (e.g., an inability to freeze rows/columns or zoom out within the workbook), it is highly recommended that you click here to open the full version of the rankings in their own separate window, as they are much easier to read that way. Within the file, there is a main left-hand section showing career stats, and as you scroll right you will see each entrant's results for each season.

Ranking Methodology: Entrants are separated into two tiers -- entrants who have played in more than one season, and those who have not -- and are then ranked by average game score.
