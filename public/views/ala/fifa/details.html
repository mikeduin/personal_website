What's the Deal? Every four years, the world unites in a fit of collective passion for the most beautiful game, and A Level Above unites to gamble on it however we possibly can. ALA offers three distinct games during the World Cup. Battle the Bookies was originally dreamt up as a pool for the World Cup and made its ALA debut as such a game in 2014 (its popularity then led to it being adapted as an annual NFL pool); also available for entry are a traditional bracket pool and an NFL-style Survivor Pool, in which an entrant must select one team each day of the tournament who must not lose to advance to the next day.
