What's the Deal? The PGA Majors Game ran from 2008-2010 but has been inactive in the years since. The premise of the game involved selecting a golfer from one of four tiers for each major; once a golfer was used in a major, he could not be used in another major that season. Golfers were then rewarded points based on how they finished in each major, and the sum of an entrant's golfers' scores for each major would be their total score for the game.
