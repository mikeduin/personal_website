What's the Deal? NCAA Pick 'em is one of the original games ... bla bla ... The rules are simple: each entrant makes 10-15 picks against the spread during each week* of the college football season; most correct picks at the end of the season wins.
Entry Open to All
Buy-in $30, $50, or $100 per entry. $25 of each $30 base buy-in goes toward the year-round standings, with the other $5 going toward prizes for each week's top score. Multiple places are paid out for each buy-in tier. For more details, see ALA Buy-ins Explained.
