What's the Deal? Though ALA Fantasy Hoops may be the symbolic heart of A Level Above, ALA Fantasy Baseball can lay claim to being its most competitive and hard-fought league in the present day. The simple 5x5 Rotiserrie format has been in place since 2005; there is no head-to-head component and there are no playoffs, and there is beauty in that simplicity. That it's extremely rare for any manager to stop tending to his roster prior to the season's final day -- despite that marathon approach to scoring -- speaks to the league's collective passion; nearly every league member has gone on record in saying it's their favorite fantasy league that they play in. Simply put, this league is special.
