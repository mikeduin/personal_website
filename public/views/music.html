Playlists
Tactfully crafted. Consistently maintained.

Music has always been the fuel that powers me through life, and these playlists are the vehicles that get me where I'm going on any given day. The seven playlists below are organized according to tempo -- from delicate and slow to hyperactive and intense -- and whether you're studying, working, exercising, or trying to turn your living room into a nightclub, there's likely something for you here (...unless your musical preferences consist solely of country music, in which case I'd advise you to run away from this page as fast as you can). I update these frequently, and I've put 10-15 of my favorite songs that I think best represent each playlist at the top of each to give you a good idea of the spoils contained therein. Beyond that, the shuffle button is your friend. Enjoy!
